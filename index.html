Phone Shop

PhoneShop is a project built with Springboot (MVC). Customers can view, look up information, place orders, give feedback to PhoneShop, log in with Google, Facebook, and pay online with Paypal Sanbox when ordering. Managers can manage customer information, manage orders placed, manage products, and statistics. In addition, the website also integrates consulting functions directly on the website and sends emails to customers.
